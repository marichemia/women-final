The Backstage of the Wilderness World.

Our zoo was founded in 2012. Our main objective is to protect and care for animals.

How it works

Every animal under our care here at the zoo is a rescue. After they are ready to go back to wilderness and their habitat we make sure to help them get back out there and live their best life! At the moment we;re helping over 50 animals of 9 different species.

Our Animals

We love to see how much joy our animals bring you and your families.

We are committed to continuing our work and helping animals and spicies while providing a unique experience and a chance to interact with animals for our visitors all year round.

In our mission to create a loving environment for our friends we need a lot of resources. Please consider donating towards our cause and help provide your favorite animals with food and necessary care.
